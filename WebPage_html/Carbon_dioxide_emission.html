Carbon dioxide emission 2010-2019
Investigation of development & developing country

Carbon dioxide (CO2) is a colourless, odourless and non-poisonous gas formed by combustion of carbon and in the respiration of living organisms. Carbon dioxide emissions or CO2 emissions are emissions stemming from the burning of fossil fuels and the manufacture of cement.

The following visualisation present the data from both development countries and developing countries carbon dioxide emission that explore from interenet, Aiming at a map,yearly emission for each country, comparsion of development & developing countries

Map Location

The map display the location of countries which are in survey, the Color of the points(Country) are represent the Sum of the emission of carbon dioxide in kilotonne(kt), then the Size of the point is their emission level. When moving the mouse on the point, the tooplip will present the value in year 2019, then with sum for ten years (2010-2019)

There are 13 countries in this survey, 7 countries are considered as development countries, others are the country in developing, by a general view, the countries that in the map is: Australia New zealand, USA, UK, China, Brazil, Ukraine, Canada, France, Netherland, Japan, Russian, Turkey.

By observing the map, although it seems to USA has almost same size(emission level) of point with Russian and China, but according to Emission in kt, China has the highest emission over ten years.

New zealand has the lowest carbon dioxide emission in there 13 countries, one should be noted that is New zealand is a development country, the significant country in development countries is USA , it contributes 54010574 kt in this ten years

Developing Vs development countries

As the pie chart shown above, there is a pie chart comparsion about developing & development countries in 10 years the developing countries are set on left hand side corner, which has the country: Turkey, Brazil, China, Russian, Netherland,Ukraine.

It's clear that data from developing country is much higher than development country, almost take up 66% of the total emission in 10 years and development countries take up 34%

China Vs World

According to the map and pie chart above, China has contributes a lot to the carbon dioxide emission not only in the sum of ten years but also splited in each year, this chart shown that each year the sum of all countries and make comparsion with China, roughly to see that China nearly half of the carbon dioxide emission for all.

Countries exploration

After get a general view, there is a colored graph for each countries and be piled up as the sum of the investigate countries emission in these ten years, the area of each countries represent their contribution to the total in each year, when put on one particular area, the graph will perform legend as a reaction.

The bar chart has directly present each country total emissions in ten years, horizontally place this country will shown a comparsion on sight, a estimate also can be made by viewing this chart.

During the Investigation of carbon dioxide emission, a trend line with detailed data for each year is imperative for analysis, the Line chart shown above is a interactive line chart, by pressing select_country button, choosing particular country to hide other country in order to further exploring.
